Introduction

A car accident/ road collision is an an event that occurs when a vehicle hits another object. This object can be another vehicle, animal, person, road debris and/or any random object. These types of events may result in damages to vehicle or object, injury, or death. Accroding to a report from the US Department of Transportation, there were about 32,719 people involved in a fatal car accident in 2013 which was 1,063 less than the 2012 report.
